Curated Experiences

Discover the Soul of Varanasi

Our carefully curated experiences invite you to explore the spiritual essence and rich cultural heritage of one of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities.

Spiritual

Private Ganga Aarti Ceremony

Witness the ancient fire ritual from an exclusive riverside setting with our expert cultural guide. As the sun sets over the Ganges, watch as Brahmin priests perform this 3,000-year-old ceremony, their synchronized movements creating a mesmerizing spectacle of light and devotion. Includes premium seating, flower offerings, and refreshments.

1.5 hours
Daily at Sunset

Dawn Boat Ride on the Ganges

Glide along the sacred waters as the first rays of sunlight illuminate the ancient ghats in shades of gold and amber. On this tranquil journey, watch as the city awakens with morning prayers, ablutions, and yoga sessions along the riverbank. A truly magical start to your day.

Daily at 5:30 AM

Try your hand at the loom and take home a small piece of your own creation.
