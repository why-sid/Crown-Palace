Welcome

A Sanctuary of Tranquility

Located near the sacred Kaal Bhairav Mandir, The Crown Palace offers a comfortable experience in the heart of Varanasi's spiritual heritage.

Our heritage property has been meticulously restored to preserve its architectural grandeur while offering world-class amenities. Each morning, witness the ethereal beauty of sunrise over the sacred river, and each evening, immerse yourself in the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti from our exclusive viewing terraces.

Whether you seek spiritual renewal, cultural immersion, or simply a serene escape, The Heritage Varanasi promises an unforgettable journey into the heart of India's most sacred city.
